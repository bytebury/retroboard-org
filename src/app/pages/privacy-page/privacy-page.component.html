Privacy Policy for Retroboard.org

Effective Date: July 01, 2024

Thank you for using Retroboard.org. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Retroboard.org collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or use our application.

Information Collection and Use

Retroboard.org uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, LLC ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about your use of the website or application. This information may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on each page
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Clickstream data
  • Demographic information (such as your age and gender, if available)
  • Interests and preferences
  • Other usage statistics

This information is collected anonymously and aggregated to understand how users interact with Retroboard.org. It helps us improve our services, content, and user experience.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that contain data specific to you and your interactions with Retroboard.org. We use cookies to:

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Customize your experience
  • Provide relevant advertisements
  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some features of Retroboard.org may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

Retroboard.org may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics) that have their own privacy policies governing the collection and use of your information. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Retroboard.org is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
